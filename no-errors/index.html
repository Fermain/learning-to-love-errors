> A world without errors
It's a simpler life.
But not without it's problems...
Without errors, we can't know when something has gone wrong...
Errors are a type of feedback that we rely on every day.
In order to do things right, we need positive and negative feedback...
We call a person who is blind to negative feedback a Narcissist
A developer cannot afford to ignore negative feedback.
Humans make mistakes often, but we are also adaptable
Computers cannot tolerate mistakes in the way we can.
They are only able to follow strict routines.
Because humans write code, there will often be mistakes...
When something unexpected happens during a routine, that is an error...
Even if the code is "perfect", things can still go wrong...
A world with no errors would be chaos.
❤️ So lets learn to love Errors
